# Image Generation Prompt - 5-Star Hotel Website Design

## Article Title (FR)
Site web hôtel 5 étoiles : pourquoi votre design coûte des réservations directes

## Article Title (EN)
5-Star Hotel Website: Why Your Design is Costing You Direct Bookings

---

## Image Prompt for Grok / Nano Banana

```
Create a luxurious, elegant illustration representing premium hotel website design and its impact on bookings.

The image should feature:
- A stunning 5-star hotel lobby or suite as the backdrop
- A sleek laptop or tablet displaying a beautiful, modern hotel website
- Before/After comparison: generic template vs luxury custom design
- Conversion funnel visualization with increasing bookings
- Design elements: elegant typography, high-quality imagery, smooth UX
- Stars or 5-star rating symbols subtly integrated
- A sense of transformation and premium upgrade
- Subtle golden accents suggesting luxury and quality

Style: Luxury hospitality, high-end web design, aspirational, suitable for upscale hotel owners.

Mood: Premium, transformative, exclusive, investment-worthy.

Color palette: Rich dark background (black or deep navy), gold/champagne accents, cream and white for elegance, subtle warm lighting.

Format: 16:9 horizontal, suitable for blog header/featured image.
```
