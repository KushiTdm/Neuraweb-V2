## Article Title (EN)
Direct Hotel Bookings: How to Recover 15 to 25% of Commissions Lost to OTAs

---

## Image Prompt for Grok / Nano Banana

```
Create a compelling business illustration representing hotel direct bookings and OTA commission savings.

The image should feature:
- A hotel building or reception desk as the central element
- Money/coins flowing back to the hotel owner instead of going to intermediaries
- A crossed-out percentage symbol (15-25%) representing saved commissions
- Booking.com/Expedia logos subtly shown as "bypassed" or "avoided"
- A direct booking path highlighted: customer → hotel website → reservation
- Growth charts showing increased revenue from direct bookings
- A smartphone or laptop displaying a hotel's own booking engine
- Safe/lock icon suggesting revenue protection

Style: Business-focused, clear value proposition, suitable for hotel owners and managers.

Mood: Profitable, independent, empowering, financially savvy.

Color palette: Deep blue background, green for money/savings, gold/yellow for revenue, white for clarity and contrast.

Format: 16:9 horizontal, suitable for blog header/featured image.
```
