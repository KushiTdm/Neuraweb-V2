## Image Prompt for Grok / Nano Banana

```
Create a dynamic, modern illustration representing AI-powered digital marketing and lead generation.

The image should feature:
- A central funnel or pipeline visual showing leads flowing through stages
- Marketing icons: email envelopes, chat bubbles, social media symbols, analytics graphs
- AI elements: robot arm or brain assisting the marketing process
- Growth arrows and upward trending charts
- Target/bullseye symbols representing lead targeting
- Multiple channels represented: email, chat, social, website
- Automation symbols: gears, connecting lines, flow arrows
- Floating data points and metrics suggesting personalization

Style: Marketing-focused, dynamic, energetic, suitable for growth hackers and marketers.

Mood: Growth-oriented, innovative, results-driven, scalable.

Color palette: Gradient background (purple to blue), orange and yellow accents for energy, white icons for clarity, green for growth indicators.

Format: 16:9 horizontal, suitable for blog header/featured image.
```
