# Image Generation Prompt - Business Process Automation ROI

## Article Title (FR)
Automatisation des processus métier : quels workflows automatiser en priorité pour un ROI maximal ?

## Article Title (EN)
Business Process Automation: Which Workflows to Prioritize for Maximum ROI?

---

## Image Prompt for Grok / Nano Banana

```
Create a professional business illustration representing ROI and process automation.

The image should feature:
- A central dashboard or analytics interface showing growth charts and metrics
- Upward trending arrows and percentage indicators suggesting positive ROI
- Clock or time-saving visual elements (hourglass, stopwatch, calendar)
- Business icons representing different processes: invoices, emails, CRM, reports
- A comparison visual showing "before" (chaotic, manual) vs "after" (streamlined, automated)
- Clean, corporate design with a sense of professionalism and trust
- Subtle automation symbols like gears, circuits, or flow arrows

Style: Business-focused, data-driven, professional, suitable for B2B decision-makers.

Mood: Profitable, strategic, time-saving, efficient.

Color palette: White/light gray background, green for positive growth, blue for trust and technology, orange accents for energy.

Format: 16:9 horizontal, suitable for blog header/featured image.
```
