# Image Generation Prompt - Hotel Website Checklist

## Article Title (FR)
Agence web pour hôtels : la checklist des 12 éléments indispensables pour un site hôtelier qui performe

## Article Title (EN)
Web Agency for Hotels: The Checklist of 12 Essential Elements for a High-Performing Hotel Website

---

## Image Prompt for Grok / Nano Banana

```
Create a premium, elegant illustration representing a high-end hotel website and digital experience.

The image should feature:
- A sleek, modern laptop or tablet displaying a beautiful hotel website interface
- Luxury hotel elements subtly integrated: elegant room interior, spa amenities, fine dining
- A floating checklist or grid showing website elements (booking engine, mobile view, reviews, photos)
- Clean, sophisticated design with a sense of exclusivity and premium quality
- Soft, warm lighting suggesting hospitality and welcome
- Abstract shapes representing technology and innovation in hospitality
- A gradient overlay suggesting sunrise/sunset (golden hour) typical of luxury travel

Style: Luxury hospitality, premium tech, elegant, aspirational, suitable for hotel industry professionals.

Mood: Sophisticated, trustworthy, high-end, exclusive.

Color palette: Deep burgundy or navy background, gold/champagne accents, cream and white for clarity, subtle warm tones.

Format: 16:9 horizontal, suitable for blog header/featured image.
```
